## Key Regulatory Developments

### SEC/CFTC Token Taxonomy (2026-03-17)

The joint taxonomy provides clear categories. Mem's classification as "Digital Tool"
depends on:
- NOT being marketed with expectations of profit from Sawalmem's efforts
- Functional utility (earned through work, spent on goods/services)
- Anti-speculation design (demurrage, no investment framing)

**Critical:** If Mem is EVER marketed with expectations of profit, it could be
reclassified as a security. This is why anti-speculation design is constitutional.

### Galette v. NJ Transit (Supreme Court 2026)

Tightens "arm-of-tribe" immunity requirements. Key implications:
- LLC operating agreement needs explicit language connecting to tribal governance
- Tribal sovereign immunity is NOT automatic for tribal enterprises
- Must demonstrate genuine governmental function, not just commercial activity

### GENIUS Act (Stablecoin)

USDC is classified as a payment stablecoin, not a security. This simplifies our
x402 settlement layer — no additional securities compliance needed for USDC payments.
